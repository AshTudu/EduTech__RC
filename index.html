About the Research Center

A multidisciplinary hub driving innovation at the intersection of education and technology.

The Center for EduTech & SkillsTech at Centurion University of Technology and Management is a dedicated research center focused on transforming education through cutting-edge technologies. Established under the Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC), the RC bridges academic research and real-world technological applications.

Our interdisciplinary team of researchers, developers, and educators collaborate on projects spanning Augmented & Virtual Reality, Blockchain-enabled Learning, Artificial Intelligence in Education, and Immersive Pedagogy. We contribute to national and international conferences, publish in Scopus- and WoS-indexed journals, file patents, and deliver hands-on training workshops.

Led by Dr. Prajna Pani (CEO) and coordinated by Mr. Abhi Mitra, the center is home to faculty and researchers from diverse disciplines — all united by the mission to make quality education more engaging, accessible, and future-ready.
